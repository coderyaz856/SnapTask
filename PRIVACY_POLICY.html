SnapTask — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

SnapTask is developed by Quordesk Labs (Morocco). This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data Stored Locally

All tasks, notes, tags, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device in a local database. This data is never uploaded to any server. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted unless you have created a backup.

2. Data Sent to Third Parties

ServiceDataPurposeProvider
Firebase Crashlytics Crash reports, device model, OS version, app version Diagnose and fix crashes Google LLC
Firebase Analytics Anonymous usage events (screen views, session duration) Understand which features are used Google LLC
Gumroad License API License key (only when you activate Pro) Verify your Pro subscription Gumroad Inc.

No personal data (name, email, location, contacts, photos) is ever collected or transmitted by SnapTask.

For details on how these third parties handle data, see: Firebase Privacy, Gumroad Privacy.

3. Permissions

PermissionWhy
InternetCrash reporting, analytics, license verification
NotificationsCore feature — task capture from notification bar, reminders
Foreground ServicePersistent notification for quick task capture

4. Backups

When you create a backup, a copy of your local database is saved to a location you choose via the Android share sheet. Backups are never uploaded automatically. You control where your data goes.

5. Data Retention

Your tasks and settings remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Crash reports and anonymous analytics are retained by Google per their standard retention policies (typically 90 days for crash data, 14 months for analytics).

6. Children's Privacy

SnapTask is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete tasks, export your data, or uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data. No account creation is required to use SnapTask.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new effective date.
